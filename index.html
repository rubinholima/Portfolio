About Me

Hello My name is Rubival Santos, but my friends call me Rubinho Lima. This is also how I am known at work. I own a production logistics company specialized in TV shows called Atrium Solutions. At age 22 I graduated with a bachelors degree in Computer Science at Universidade Salvador, in Brazil. My first employment experience was with the IT department of Emilio Odebrecht Foundation, a well-known organization in Brazil. After just a few months of employment, I was selected to work for a local TV station called TV Bahia, in the IT support area. While there I used to spend my lunch break everyday near the journalism production department. I gradually used my computer skills to improve the broadcasting of evening news stories throughout the State of Bahia.

My work was soon recognized and I was transferred to the largest television network in Brazil. I spent most of my career in Brazil working for Rede Globo. There I worked with the production of soap operas, entertainment and sports shows, as well as the transmission of soccer matches and F-1 races. In 2000 I moved with my family to the United States where I continued to work with production logistics. As I grew older and more experienced, I decided that it was time to return to my professional origins. So today I am back at school to study Computer Programming. I am very pleased to be part of a reputable university, studying what I like and feeling blessed to have all the support of the teachers, TA's, the staff. With their support I will reach my career goals. I can only be grateful and be proud of it!
